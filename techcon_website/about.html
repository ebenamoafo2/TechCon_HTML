Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of passionate developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential global technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants annually.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and provide a platform for the brightest minds in technology to share ideas that shape the future. We believe in empowering individuals and communities through knowledge and creativity.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology:

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Recognized as one of the first computer programmers, Dr. Lovelace inspired attendees with her insights on the evolution of computing and its impact on society.

Alan Turing

Known for his pioneering work in artificial intelligence and computer science, Turing shared his vision of ethical computing and problem-solving.

Grace Hopper

Rear Admiral Hopper inspired generations with her groundbreaking contributions to computer programming languages and innovation in software development.
